Horizon - Premium luxurious 4 berth

4 BERTH

Perfect for 2 couples or small families wanting a Dual aspect daytime living

The Horizon Motorhome is based upon a 2022 Fiat Ducato RollerTeam T-line 700 sleeping 4 people.

This multifunctional, compact motorhome, delivers exceptional drive quality thanks to its 9 speed Automatic gearbox and cutting-edge layout. Fitting to both couples and small families, the four berth T-Line 700 features a dual aspect living area maximising the space available during the day, whilst the design & materials used create a truly luxurious feel.

Fitting for both couples and small families, this four-berth benefits from stunning interior design with, wood and upholstery including a fabric covered ceiling for a ‘soft touch’ feeling.

Featuring a pivoting wall that divides the shower and toilet areas; this adapts the space for each purpose to maximise the room available. The popular and well laid out L- shaped kitchen gives ample space to cook and with the elegant 3 burner hob you can cook a delicious meal in confidence after your day of adventures. When twilight falls, this compact motorhome features two double electrical operated drop-down beds along with lights above each side with USB plugs, too.

This motorhome comes with a simple to use control panel for everything onboard whilst there is a free unlimited Wi-Fi onboard which works seamlessly with the latest Avtex Smart TV. Packed with all new features you’d expect from a much larger model, Horizon is a modern, innovative motorhome.

There is a full kitchen, including 3 burner gas hob, gas oven, sink and larger than average fridge with freezer compartment.

Hot water to all taps and shower whilst a hot air distribution system provides central heating. Fly screens and blackout blinds are available for all windows and habitation door.

There are 4 travelling 3 point seat belts.

Reversing camera fitted to help manoeuvring, alarm & tracker also fitted.
