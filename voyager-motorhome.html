Perfect for families or those wanting bunk beds

Our Voyager Motorhomes are based upon a 2020 Ford Transit with Automatic gearbox. The RollerTeam 675 sleeping upto 6 people layout consists of a large over cab double bed accessed via a ladder, 2 very big single bunk beds the top bunk is accessed via ladder and a dinette which converts into a small double.

They are extremely popular with families and friends going camping or to festivals. There is a large rear garage that is perfect for storing all outdoor equipment like skis and surfboards. The bottom bunk can easily be folded up if not required to increase storage space even further. There is a full kitchen, including 3 burner gas hob, gas oven, sink and larger than average fridge with freezer compartment.

In the bathroom is a separate shower, toilet with electric flush and basin. Hot water to all taps and shower whilst a hot air distribution system provides central heating. Fly screens and blackout blinds are available for all windows and habitation door.

Reversing camera fitted to help manoeuvring, tracker also fitted. The rear contains flexible under bed storage area accessible from outside. There are 6 travelling 3 point seat belts, including 2 Isofix seats in the forward facing rear seats.
