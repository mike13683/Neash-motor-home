About NICHE MOTORHOME HIRE

Welcome to Niche Motorhome Hire. We are a family owned business based near Loughborough who having taken so much pleasure from our own camping trips, we decided to give others the joy of using our motorhomes. As such all our vehicles come with everything you need to have a great trip no matter what the weather. Having hired motorhomes ourselves before buying our own we know what things were often missing so ours our loaded with things not usually found in hired Motorhomes. We also hated all those hidden extras so wanted to be transparent and provide a comprehensive motorhome with only a handful of optional extras.

We believe in 100% Customer Satisfaction – We Live by It!

The easiest way to hire from us is, simply fill in the form on the booking page and then sit back & wait for us to confirm your requirements, alternatively you can continue to complete the booking yourself answering the required questions and entering your payment details. If still unsure then please email us using the link at the top of the page instead.

Once your booking deposit is received and confirmed your hire is secured we will then just need a copy of your driving licence and proof of address for the insurance a couple of weeks before you hire commences. You’re then set for your motorhome hire adventure to begin.

We have listed some ‘Things to do’ below for you to explore but these are just for starters!
