WHAT’S INCLUDED WITH YOUR MOTORHOME HIRE?

We also hated all those hidden extras so wanted to be transparent and provide a comprehensive motorhome with only a handful of optional extras. So we have kitted out our vans to a very high specification. You will find everything necessary to make the most of your holiday. Pet Policy

    In addition to the standard terms and conditions, the following rules apply when a pet is present in our motorhome:

  • Prior written consent has been obtained from Niche Motorhome Hire via the booking form.
  • An increased security deposit is paid.
  • The vehicle is returned in a clean and tidy condition as if a pet was never present. Should this not be the case, or any pet hairs are found, cleaning charges and upholstery cleaning charges will apply. Minimum charge is £100.00.
  • Pets are not left in the vehicle unattended.
  • Pets are not allowed on any of the soft furnishings or upholstery (this includes the beds, seats/sofas)
  • You agree to cover the cost of all damage caused by a pet, even if it exceeds the security deposit.
