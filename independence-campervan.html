Independence Camper 4 birth

5 BERTH

Perfect for couples & families or those wanting home comforts

An ideal campervan for those who enjoy entertaining home comforts but want the independence to go almost anywhere, this Camper benefits from have a pop up roof and also features separate rear seats that can either be made into 2 single or a double bed when using the swivel captain seats.

The Volkswagen T6.1 Cromford moves away from the traditional campervan conversion, using a touch of innovation and expert craftmanship this helps creates a more luxurious experience. Crafted on a LWB with 7 speed DSG automatic gearbox. In Day Mode- the ergonomic seats provide a comfortable seating & dining area, while the rear galley style kitchen provides all the storage and equipment you could need. In Night mode- The rear seats fold completely flat in just two simple movements, forming either a comfortable double bed or two singles. Black out blinds provide privacy while LED lighting keeps the camper cosy and well lit. There is even a compact bathroom at the rear which includes a flushing cassette toilet and basin. Hot and cold water is available when on a hookup.

It is extremely popular with couples, families and friends going camping or to festivals.

There is a full kitchen, including 2 burner gas hob, gas oven, sink and a 50ltr fridge with freezer compartment.

Hot water (only whilst on a hookup) to all taps and external activity shower whilst heating is provided by a diesel heater. Fly screens and blackout blinds are available for all windows and doors.

There are 4 travelling 3 point seat belts.

Reversing camera fitted to help manoeuvring, alarm & tracker also fitted.
