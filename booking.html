How to use our booking page

We are delighted that you have chosen to book a vehicle or would like a quote from us. Remember our prices include insurance, roadside assistance, telephone support whilst on hire as well as comprehensive range of home from home comforts.

You have 2 options to use our booking system, by Date or by Motorhome

Date option (shows which Motorhomes are available for your dates)

1. Click on the “Date” button.

2. Select the dates you wish your hire to start and end on.

3. Click on “Apply” at the bottom of the screen.

4. Click on the motorhome you wish to hire. (Unavailable motorhomes will be greyed out).

5. Enter your name, email & Phone no. then press 'Reserve Now'

Motorhome option (shows which dates are available for your selected Motorhome)

1. Click on the motorhome you wish to hire.

2. Select the dates you wish your hire to start and end on.

3. Enter your name, email & Phone no. then press 'Reserve Now'

Not ready to reserve / book or should you wish to speak to us for more information please don’t hesitate to contact us. If your required dates do not appear available on the booking calendar then please contact us on hello@n-mh.co.uk or 01509 426442 and we will do our best to accommodate you.
